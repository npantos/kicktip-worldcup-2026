## Method notes accumulated this month

1. **Market anchoring beat the Elo model over the tournament as a whole** — but the
   two semifinals were both Elo wins over the market. Hypothesis for next time: anchor
   to market through the group stage and early knockouts, blend 50/50 from the QF on.
2. **Platform grading rules are alpha.** The after-pens correction (shootout graded as
   120' goals + shootout goals; drawn scorelines can never land) changed EP-max on
   every knockout line and was verified against the platform, not assumed.
3. **Totals-aware lambda fitting.** Outcome probabilities under-determine the score
   matrix; when Over/Under prices imply a different total than the fit, re-fit. It
   flipped the bronze-final pick correctly (10 goals!) even though the side missed.
4. **Re-bet protocol works.** Both deliberate pre-kickoff re-bets (pens-grading
   correction; Balogun reinstatement) were positive-EV at the time with full sourcing
   in `posted_to`. One gained, one cost 2 points — that's what fair coin edges look
   like in small samples.
5. **Sensitivity checks before late re-bets.** Several times a lineup wrinkle tempted
   a change and a ±4pp probability haircut showed the pick was robust. Cheap
   discipline, repeatedly saved churn.
6. **Bonus questions resolve late and quietly.** An empty bonus-question API response
   means locked, not settled. Track pending season-long questions explicitly in the
   standings notes — they moved more points in the endgame than any single match bet.
